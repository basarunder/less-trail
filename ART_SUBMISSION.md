# PROBABILITY TERMINAL: A Generative Syntax Experiment

**Artist:** Başar Ünder  
**Year:** 2025  
**Medium:** Web-based Generative Software (p5.js, Datamuse API, Custom ASCII algorithms)

---

### Concept
*Probability Terminal* is a digital study in chance, constraint, and meaning. Reimagining the analog fate of dice through a stark, retro-futurist lens, the work transforms a simple numerical outcome into a lexical event.

Upon interaction, the system casts two virtual dice, their values determining not a score, but the strict structural constraints of a new reality: the length of words. A 3 and a 6 do not equal 9; they birth a three-letter seed and a six-letter anchor (e.g., "EGO MANIAC"). The resulting phrases—pulled from the vast, chaotic lexicon of the Datamuse API—range from profound to absurd, creating a fleeting poignant moment before they are wiped away for the next roll.

Presented in a borderless, immersive void using dot-matrix typography, the piece invites the viewer to act as the agent of chaos, endlessly rolling for meaning in a system governed by cold logic.

### Technical Statement
The work is built on the **p5.js** creative coding framework, utilizing a custom-built rendering engine to display responsive ASCII graphics. The visual language is anchored by the **"Doto"** typeface, evoking the heritage of early computing and departure boards.

Beneath the visual layer, the system interfaces with the **Datamuse API** in real-time. It does not simply pick random words; it queries a linguistic database using the specific numerical constraints generated by the dice, filtering for semantic coherence while allowing for surreal juxtaposition.

### Viewing Instructions
1.  **Interact:** Tap or click anywhere on the terminal screen to initiate the sequence.
2.  **Observe:** Watch the probability collapse as the dice settle and the machine searches for a matching phrase.
3.  **Repeat:** There is no win state. The cycle of generation is the artwork.
